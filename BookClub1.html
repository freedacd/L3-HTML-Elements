Book 1 - Me Tawk Funny – Heroes, villains, and a reason to study

In, Me Tawk Funny, another well-told tale from Neil McFarlane, our main character finds a good reason to study in this tale of heroes and villains. Would you think you were crazy if your dog suddenly started talking? Buster initially talks a bit funny, but slowly better and better.

Book 2 - Johnny Jetpack

Johnny Johnson always dreamed of one day flying through space. When his inventive grandfather builds him a Jetpack for his birthday, he grows as close to obtaining that dream as any small boy can ever hope.

Book 3 - How to Draw Supercow

How to Draw Supercow – This is a craft book for children who love Supercow to practise drawing him themselves, it is designed as a compliment to the Supercow series for printing and practising and so is not available as a hard copy at the moment.
